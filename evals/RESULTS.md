## Notes
- Coverage includes persistent ability state, personal relationship cues, consent gates, correction handling, context salience, and context-window packing.
- Baselines intentionally lack structured memory, policy checks, and cue-ladder state; their failures identify where ReVoice adds architecture beyond transcript search.
- Results are generated by running this script against the local scoring and baseline code.

### Dataset provenance

- **`_CATEGORY_EXPANSIONS` vocabulary** derived from Princeton WordNet (Miller, 1995) via NLTK, filtered by wordfreq >= 1e-6 (Speer et al., 2018 — sourced from Wikipedia, OpenSubtitles, Twitter). Derivation script: `data/validation/derive_expansions_wordnet.py`. Raw output: `data/validation/wordnet_expansions.json`.
- **PNT error taxonomy** from Schwartz et al. (2006) and Roach et al. (1996). 175-item target word list: `data/validation/pnt_targets.json`.
- **Cue ladder** grounded in Linebaugh & Lehner (1977). Full citation map: `docs/clinical_grounding.md`.
